My Photography

Four basic rules to keep in mind:

1. Rule of third: Keep your object in any one of the intersection lines of the grid.
It creates viewer to subject connection.

2. Leading Lines: focus on the leading lines which point to your object.

3. Centered Composition: This type of composition works amazing when you have some kind of symmetry or you want to show some reflection.

4. Foreground interest: foreground which actually complements the scene i.e. to draw the attention of the viewer from the foreground to background - which in a way may tell a story.
